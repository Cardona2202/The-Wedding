Our Love Story

How our journey began

2009

Where It All Began

It all started with a simple text message. Introduced by childhood friends, we became each other’s constant until life briefly pulled us apart.

2010

The First Meeting

Fate found its way back through family and friends. On May 23, 2010, during a town fiesta, we finally met in person—marking the beginning of something real.

2011

Young Love

We became official—quietly and secretly. From quick meet-ups to simple dates at Jollibee or McDonald’s to Kinalas or Tuhog-tuhog, love grew in the smallest, most genuine moments.

2016

A New Chapter

We welcomed our greatest blessing—our child, Renesmark Deon. We weren’t ready, but love made us strong enough to become the family we are today.

2019

Time Apart

Differences and growing pains led us down separate paths. Distance and time shaped us into who we needed to become.

2021

Finding Our Way Back

On December 2, love knocked again. By Christmas, we chose each other once more—this time, forever.

2026

Forever Begins

After everything—every high and low—we are here. Still choosing each other. Still fighting for love. And now, finally… forever.
